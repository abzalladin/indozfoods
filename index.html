What are pomegranates?

Pomegranates are round, reddish-brown fruits with a hard, smooth exterior.Pomegranate seeds can be eaten fresh as a sweet-tart snack, used as a garnish or ingredient, or dried.

Benefits of Pomegranate?

Pomegranate has anti-oxidant, anti-viral and anti-tumor properties and is said to be a good source of vitamins, especially vitamin A, vitamin C, and vitamin E, as well as folic acid.

What variety do we offer?

We import the best pomegrantes which have a bigger fruit size, sweet, bold and attractive arils, glossy, very attractive saffron coloured thick skin.

What else?

We also import the best Mango and Guava.
